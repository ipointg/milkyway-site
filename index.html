The Journey

An ancient road. An eternal sky.

Centuries ago, Chumaks — Ukrainian salt traders — crossed the endless steppe with their ox-drawn wagons, navigating by the stars. Their road was hard, their cargo precious, and their stories became legend. Now you walk in their footsteps.

Strategy RPG Resource Management Historical Atmospheric Story Rich Pixel Graphics Single-player
